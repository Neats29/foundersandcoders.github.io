The application process

  • Join our mailing list
  • Application form, Part 1 (the shorter one)
  • Application form, Part 2 (the longer one)

You need to join our mailing list to make sure that you receive updates. Part 2 of the application requires that you complete a short video.

Things you can do to improve your chances of getting on the course

  • Spend some time learning on Codecademy
  • Tell your friends about us (and get them to mention your name when they apply)
  • Tweet about us

Above all, try to communicate through your application that you are capable of handling the workload and are committed to using the skills you learn with us after you graduate and also to helping us publicise and develop the course.

Which Codecademy courses do you recommend?

  • Make a website
  • Make an interactive website
  • HTML & CSS
  • jQuery
  • JavaScript
  • YouTube API

You do not have to finish all these courses before applying, but doing so will help make the case for your capacity to finish the course.
