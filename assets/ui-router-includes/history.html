When were you founded?

December 2014.

Who is the founder?

Dan Sofer, a long-time software developer and tech industry veteran.

Who runs the company?

A social enterprise (CIC) set up by Dan and by students and alumni of a series of courses that ran in 2014 at Collective in Camden Town.

For how long did the courses run in Camden Town?

Four cohorts of students graduated from a series of 6-8 week coding courses between january and November 2014.

What were the student outcomes?

On the most recent course, of 16 students who signed up, 15 completed the course, 3 found jobs on graduation or shortly after and 10 are working on a variety of paid post-graduate projects, including a major new website for Camden Council and wep apps for two early-stage startups.
