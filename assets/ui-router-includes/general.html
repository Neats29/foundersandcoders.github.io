Who are Founders & Coders?

We are a community of software developers and educators.

What is the Founders & Coders programme?

It is a 8-week course of study in software development for the Web.

What is the aim of the programme?

To give students with no previous technical experience sufficient skills to find employment as a software developer.

Who is the programme for?

Anyone over the age of 18 who is self-motivated and serious about becoming a software developer.

Where is the programme based?

Our new campus in Mile End.

How much does it cost?

There are no fees.

How do you manage to make it free?

We have crowdfunded the first few months’ rent and we don’t pay the teachers.

Is it any good?

Many of our students think so. You can read some of their stories here.

If this programme is so good, why are you offering it for free?

We are trying to build a community and we do not think we can do that by charging an entry fee.
